Masala fried pomfret fish

serving:6

A very easy to make masala fish marinated in a range of spices and coated in semolina before frying. Served with a sweet and tangy warm cabbage and kiwi salad.

Ingredients

    For the fish
  • 1 number pomfret
  • Semolina/ Suj to coat
  • Oil to shallow fry
    • For the masala
  • 2 tablespoon Ginger
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
  • 1 number Juice of lemon
    • For the warm cabbage and kiwi salad
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon Mustard seeds
  • 1 Red chilli slit
  • 1 green chilli slit
  • 2-3 numbers Curry Leaves
  • 1 number Spring onion sliced diagonally
  • 1/2 cup cabbage shredded
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • 1 Kiwi
  • 1/2 tablespoon Vinegar
  • sugar a pinch
  • Grated coconut, to garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

    For the masala
  1. In a blender mix together ginger, garluic, oil, turmeric, red chilli and lemon juice.
  2. Masala is ready.
    3. For the fish
  3. Make gashes in the pomfret and apply the masala.
  4. Marinate it for 15 minutes.
  5. Coat it in semolina from both sides and shallow fry till golden.
  6. Serve with warm cabbage and kiwi salad.
    7. For the warm cabbage and kiwi salad
  7. In a pan add oil, mustard seeds, red and green chilli, curry leaves. Let them splutter.
  8. Add spring onions. Saute.
  9. Add cabbage. Saute and add salt and pepper.
  10. Turn off the heat.
  11. Add diced kiwi, vinegar and sugar.
  12. Mix well and let it cook in the pan's residual heat.
  13. Garnish with grated coconut.
  14. Serve with masala fried fish.
