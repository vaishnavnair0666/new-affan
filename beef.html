serving:6

Laal Maas is a very popular non-veg recipe in Rajasthan. It is a fiery meat curry which is made with the combination of Kashmiri red chillies.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Grind the soaked chillies to a fine paste in a blender.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mutton, yogurt, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, one tablespoon ginger garlic paste and the red chilli paste.
  3. Mix well and set aside to marinate in the refrigerator for an hour.
  4. Heat ghee in a nonstick pressure cooker, to that add black cardamom, green cardamoms, cinnamon, cloves and bay leaf and saut� till its fragrant.
  5. Add the onions and saut� till they turn light brown in colour. Then add the remaining ginger garlic paste and saut� for a minute.
  6. Add the marinated mutton to the onions and saut� on high heat for two to three minutes.
  7. Add salt and sufficient water to cover all the mutton pieces.
  8. Cover with a lid and let it cook under pressure for seven to eight whistles.
  9. Once done, remove from heat and wait till the pressure is released.
  10. Meanwhile light the piece of charcoal, place it in a small metal bowl and place the bowl in the centre of pressure cooker.
  11. Add two to three cloves to the coal, pour some ghee and quickly cover with a lid and set aside for two to three minutes.
  12. Put the curry back on heat, add garam masala and give a quick stir before removing from heat.
  13. Transfer to a serving bowl, garnish with coriander sprigs and a lemon wedge. Serve hot.
