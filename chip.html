CHOCO CHIPS

Sweet? Check. Salty? Check. Spicy? Check. (Well, just a little.) This three-ingredient bark made with chocolate, tortilla chips and chile flakes is a seriously addictive treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 12 - ounce package semisweet chocolate pieces
  • 2 cups finely crushed corn tortilla chips*
  • 1 -2 teaspoons crushed red pepper (optional)

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, microwave chocolate for 1 minute. Stir. Continue to microwave and stir at 20-second intervals, until smooth. Stir in tortilla chips. Spread or pat mixture on a parchment-lined baking sheet to a 12x8-inch rectangle (mixture will be thick). Sprinkle with crushed red pepper, if desired. Freeze 15 minutes. Break into pieces. Store in refrigerator up to 5 days.
