Chingri Macher Malaikar Recipe

Serving:6

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kilograms Prawns
  • 2 Cinnamon cinnamon stick
  • 6 cup green cardamom
  • 2 tablespoon Ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder
  • 2 teaspoon ghee
  • 1 number lime
  • 1 tablespoon lemon rinds
  • 150 ml refined oil
  • 4 number clove
  • 12 tablespoon cumin
  • 2 teaspoon Cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 cup coconut milk
  • 500 gm Basmati rice
  • 6 leaves kaffir lime leaves
  • 1/2 cup hot water

    • For Marination

  • 4 number onion
  • 1 teaspoon powdered turmeric
  • 2 tablespoon crushed to paste cumin
  • 2 pinches Salt

    • For Garnishing

  • 6 sprigs chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Coconut flake

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. For making the Chingri Malaikari, clean the prawns with warm salted water and marinate with 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder and 1/4 pinch of salt along with a pinch of onion, ginger and cumin seeds.
  2. Let the prawns marinade.
  3. Prepare an onion-ginger paste in a mixer.
  4. Then heat ghee in a kadai or a deep pan and fry the prawns till they turn golden brown, and keep them aside in a bowl.
  5. Put another frying pan on low-medium flame and heat oil in it add cinnamon, cardamom, cloves and bay leaves in it, let all the spices crackle.
  6. Add the paste of onion and ginger and fry it on medium flame for about 4-5 minutes.
  7. Then add remaining turmeric powder, kashmiri chilli powder, salt and garam masala to the frying onions and saute for a few seconds.
  8. Now, add coconut milk and 1/2 cup of water and bring it to boil.
  9. Then cut the green chillies and add them to the gravy along with the fried prawns.
  10. Allow it to cook for another 5-6 minutes. Put off the flame, garnish the chingrir malaikari with coriander leaves and add some coconut flakes on top of it.
  11. Keep it aside.
  12. Now, for the Gondhoraaj rice, soak the basmati rice for half an hour in water in a large vessel, and keep them aside.
  13. Put water in a pan as per requirement and bring it to boil.
  14. Then add a pinch of salt, one spoon of lime juice, one small piece of lemon rind, two kaffir lime leaves and one spoon of ghee to the boiling water, and add the soaked rice.
  15. Serve the cooked Gondhoraaj (lime) rice with malaikari.
